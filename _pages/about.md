Passionate researcher with over 10 years of professional experience in academia and industry. Developer of a genome engineering platform for genetic screens in human primary T cells. Currently leading a large team project to engineer next generation T cell-based immunotherapies via CRISPR screens. My long-term goal is to develop technologies to explore and translate innovative therapies.
